Questions

Good to know.

Is my data really private? +
Yes, Every capsule is encrypted with AES-256-GCM before it ever leaves your device. Even we can't read your memories, only you and the people you invite hold the keys.
What if I forget my password? +
Because encryption is end-to-end, your password is the key. We recommend keeping it somewhere safe. A reset flow is on the roadmap for a future update.
Can I invite more than one person? +
Yes, capsules are collaborative. Invite anyone by username and they can add memories before the capsule is sealed. Everyone unlocks it together on the chosen date.
When is the Android version coming? +
Boxed is launching on iOS first. Android is actively planned and will follow after the initial launch. Join the waitlist and you'll be the first to know.
Is Boxed free to use? +
The core experience will be free. Pricing details will be shared on launch day — waitlist members will get the best early access deal.
